About Us

Welcome to ATLAS Student Council, it is an organisation composed of its elected and nominated members by the students of Dolna Day School. ATLAS has ensured that the students from different classes achieve better connectivity, strengthen ties and work co-operatively to improve the efficiency of Students' Council.

Our History

ATLAS Student Council was created on 26th July, 2024, after months of tedious campaigning by students from Classes VI to X. The students who composed te team of members in the session 2024-25 came to be known as the Founding Members. Within 8 months of its creation, ATLAS consistently delivered 3 grand events of exceptional magnitude. The first ever intra-class debate competition of Dolna Day School,"Agree to Disagree", first ever Model United Nations and first ever Talent Show, "Dolna's Got Talent". They were all first of their kinds and were a huge success. Within 8 months, ATLAS has solidified its exceptional and legendary status.

Our Mission

The ATLAS Student Council aims to provide the students of Dolna Day School a chance and ability to make their own destiny. It was created with the goal of letting the students have a say in the events and competitions, they want to have and the goal remains the same. With new projects, ATLAS is becoming more than just events it is now a place where students can truly have a say on the matters which concern them.

What We Do

ATLAS Student Council originally prioritised in events and their executions. Over time, it had transformed itself as the apex student body in Dolna Day School emerging as the management body of the various acitivity clubs created by students.
Atlas Student Council will deliver promising and fabulous events throughout the year for the students to enjoy and also help manage the various different clubs for the students to engage on a weekly basis
